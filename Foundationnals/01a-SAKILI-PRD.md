# 1. Executive Overview

SAKILI is a continental AI ecosystem infrastructure platform headquartered in Mauritius and designed to become the trusted gateway for discovering, connecting, and collaborating with African AI ecosystems.

The platform serves as:
- a continental AI registry,
- an AI ecosystem discovery engine,
- a collaboration network,
- an intelligence platform,
- and a trust infrastructure layer.

SAKILI is not a traditional marketplace focused on transaction extraction.

Instead, it is designed as:
# ecosystem coordination infrastructure.

The platform prioritizes:
- visibility,
- accessibility,
- inclusion,
- discoverability,
- trust,
- and intra-African collaboration.

Primary target users include:
- AI SMEs,
- nano businesses,
- startups,
- AI consultants,
- universities,
- innovation hubs,
- governments,
- investors,
- and international ecosystem partners.

# 4. Core Product Philosophy

## Key Principles

### Inclusion First
The platform must remain accessible to:
- micro businesses,
- startups,
- students,
- researchers,
- and underrepresented ecosystems.

### Low-Friction Participation
The onboarding process should be extremely simple and low-cost.

### Ecosystem Before Extraction
SAKILI does not prioritize commissions or transactional monetization.

### Trust & Verification
The platform should progressively become a trusted infrastructure layer.

### Pan-African Identity
The platform must feel:
- African,
- multilingual,
- collaborative,
- and ecosystem-centric.

### Mobile-First Accessibility
Most African users will access the platform via mobile.
